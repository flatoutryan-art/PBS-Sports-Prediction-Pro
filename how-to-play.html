Welcome to PBS Picks Pro — the closed-league World Cup 2026 prediction game for the PBS crew. Predict the score of every match, rack up points, and climb the leaderboard. The person with the most points when the final whistle blows wins the glory (and bragging rights until 2030).

Scoring System
3
⚡ Exact Score
You predict the exact final score — both home and away goals.
1
✓ Correct Result
You predicted the right outcome (home win, draw, or away win) but not the exact scoreline.
0
✗ Wrong Pick
Your predicted outcome doesn't match the final result.
Example: Brazil vs Argentina. You predict 2–1 Brazil. Final score: 2–0 Brazil. You get +1 pt — correct result (Brazil won), but not the exact score. If you'd predicted 2–0, you'd have scored +3 pts.
How to Submit a Prediction
1

Open the Fixtures tab and find an upcoming match.

2

Tap the match card to expand it.

3

Enter your predicted home and away scores using the number inputs.

4

Tap "Lock In Prediction" before the match kicks off.

5

The gold border on the card confirms your prediction is saved. You can edit it any time before kickoff.

FAQ
When can I submit predictions?
Any time before the match kicks off. Once the whistle blows, predictions are locked and you can't make changes. Submit early — don't get caught out.
Can I change my prediction?
Yes — as many times as you like, right up until kickoff. Tap the saved card again and update your scores.
When are points awarded?
The admin enters the final score after each match. Points appear on the leaderboard within minutes of full time — usually before you finish your post-match analysis.
What if I forget to predict a match?
You score 0 for that match. The Fixtures tab will show it as "No prediction submitted". Use the WhatsApp alerts to avoid missing kickoff.
How do I get WhatsApp reminders?
Go to Settings → enter your mobile number. You'll get a reminder 4 hours before every match and a weekly standings update every Sunday at 18:00.
What if a match is postponed?
The admin updates the kickoff time. Your prediction stays saved and the lock time shifts with the new schedule.
Who can see my predictions?
Before kickoff, your predictions are private. After the match is locked you can see everyone's picks on the My Picks page — no peeking before kickoff.
How do I sign in?
Enter your registered WhatsApp number. First-time? You'll be prompted to set a 6-digit PIN. After that, it's just your number + PIN — no password needed.
